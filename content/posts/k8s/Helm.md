Imagine **Helm** for **Kubernetes** just like `apt` on **Ubuntu**, or "App Store" on **iPhone**

In short: **Helm** is a "Package Manager" for **Kubernetes**

## 1. Why do we need Helm ?

When work with K8s, you must write so much YAML file (Deployment, Service, Ingress, ConfigMap,...)
